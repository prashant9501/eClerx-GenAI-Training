# ShopSmart Customer Support Policies

## Return & Refund Policy

### Return Window
- Standard items: 30 days from delivery date
- Electronics: 15 days from delivery date
- Final sale items: No returns accepted

### Return Conditions
- Item must be in original condition
- Original packaging required
- Receipt or order number required
- Return shipping: Customer pays unless item is defective

### Refund Processing
- Refunds processed within 5-7 business days after receiving returned item
- Original payment method will be credited
- Shipping costs are non-refundable unless item was defective

### Defective/Damaged Items
- Full refund including shipping costs
- Replacement offered at no additional cost
- Must report within 48 hours of delivery

## Shipping Policy

### Standard Shipping
- 5-7 business days
- Free on orders over $50
- $5.99 for orders under $50

### Express Shipping
- 2-3 business days
- $14.99 flat rate

### Tracking
- Tracking number provided within 24 hours of shipment
- Track at: shopsmarttrack.com

## Billing & Payment Policy

### Accepted Payment Methods
- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover)
- PayPal
- Shop Pay
- Apple Pay

### Billing Issues
- Double charges: Contact support immediately, resolved within 24 hours
- Incorrect amount: Verify cart total, contact support with order number
- Failed payment: Verify card details, try alternative payment method

## Order Modification & Cancellation

### Order Cancellation
- Can cancel within 1 hour of placing order
- Orders in processing cannot be cancelled (contact support for case-by-case review)
- Refund processed within 3-5 business days

### Order Modification
- Address changes: Within 1 hour of placing order
- Item changes: Not possible after order is placed (must cancel and reorder)

## Customer Service Escalation Criteria

### Immediate Escalation Required
- Customer tier: Platinum (always escalate)
- Complaint mentions: Legal action, social media threat, regulatory complaint
- Repeat contact: 3+ contacts on same issue
- High-value dispute: Orders over $500

### Manager Review Required
- Refund requests over $200
- Policy exceptions
- Abusive language toward support staff

## Technical Support

### Account Access Issues
- Password reset: Self-service via "Forgot Password"
- Account locked: Support can unlock after identity verification
- Email not received: Check spam folder, verify email address on file

### Website Issues
- Payment errors: Try alternative payment method, clear browser cache
- Site performance: Recommended browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari latest versions)

## Special Circumstances

### Lost Packages
- Delivered per tracking but not received: File claim after 2 business days
- Investigation period: 5-7 business days
- Resolution: Refund or replacement

### Incorrect Items Received
- Full refund including return shipping
- Keep or return incorrect item (support will advise)
- Replacement sent via express shipping at no cost
